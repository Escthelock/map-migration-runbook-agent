# Return on Investment Report

## AnyCompany Healthcare — AWS Cloud Migration

### Prepared for: Executive Leadership Team

---

## Executive Summary

Migrating AnyCompany's 20 applications and 800TB+ infrastructure to AWS delivers a projected **5-year net benefit of $4.9M** with a **285% ROI** and **payback in 18 months**. Annual run-rate savings of $1.4M begin in Year 2, growing to $1.8M by Year 5 through continued optimisation and licence elimination.

## Cost of Inaction

Remaining on-premises carries escalating risk:

| Risk | Financial Exposure | Likelihood (3-Year) |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Hardware failure (aging servers, avg 12 years) | $500K–$2M unplanned outage | High |
| Oracle/VMware licence audit | $200K–$1M compliance penalty | Medium |
| HIPAA breach (manual controls) | $1.5M–$10M fine + remediation | Low–Medium |
| Inability to scale for clinical demand | Revenue loss, patient safety risk | Medium |
| EOL security vulnerability (Java 8, COBOL) | $500K–$5M breach cost | Medium |
| Hardware refresh (Year 3 mandatory) | $2.2M capital expenditure | Certain |

**Expected cost of inaction (3-year): $3.5M–$5M in risk exposure + $2.2M mandatory hardware refresh**

---

## Recommendation

Proceed with the AWS migration programme. The investment pays back in 18 months and delivers $4.9M net benefit over 5 years while eliminating $5M+ in technical and compliance risk. Every month of delay adds ~$120K in avoidable on-premises costs and extends exposure to EOL security vulnerabilities.
