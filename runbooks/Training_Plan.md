## 1. Training Objectives

| Phase | Objective | Outcome |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| Assess | Build cloud literacy across IT organisation | Team understands AWS fundamentals, can participate in discovery workshops |
| Mobilize | Develop hands-on migration skills | Team can execute pilot migrations, operate landing zone, manage IaC |
| Migrate & Modernize | Achieve operational independence | Customer team operates AWS environment without partner dependency |
