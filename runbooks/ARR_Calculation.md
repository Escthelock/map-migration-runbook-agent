## 7. Assumptions & Notes

- Daily rates based on mid-market AWS consulting partner ($1,500/day blended)
- Team sizes reflect AnyCompany complexity (20 apps, HIPAA, 800TB data)
- AWS resale margin assumes partner is in AWS Partner Network with margin programme
- Managed services pricing assumes 24/7 coverage with shared NOC model
- Modernization sprints priced at $75K each (2 per year in Years 1–2, 1 in Year 3)
- ARR excludes professional services (one-time) per standard SaaS/services ARR definition
- Smaller customers (5–10 apps) would yield ~$300K–$500K ARR at similar margins

---

*This model represents a single AnyCompany-scale engagement. Partner ARR scales linearly with customer count.*
